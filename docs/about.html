"Grads of London began as a idea by two former graduates in the
UK who took part in a coding project. The initial goal was to create an
extensive catalogue of London's graduates."




"We wanted to make these stories as realistic as possible to outline
the different paths that students have took since graduation. We
wanted empower students across the globe with these stories, so
they know that everyone is unique and there is no 'right' path to take
after university."
